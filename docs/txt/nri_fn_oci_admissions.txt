NRI / FN / OCI / CIWG Quota Admissions – VNRVJIET
==================================================

Seat Category
-------------
NRI (Non-Resident Indian), FN (Foreign National), OCI (Overseas Citizens of India), and CIWG (Children of Indian Workers in Gulf) seats are offered at VNRVJIET under the NRI/Foreign category quota.

Eligibility Criteria
--------------------
Admissions under the Foreign category are open to candidates who meet ANY of the following conditions:

1. **OCI Card Holders:**
   - The student is an OCI (Overseas Citizen of India) cardholder.
   - Proof required: Passport + OCI card of the student.

2. **CIWG Category (Children of Indian Workers in Gulf):**
   - The student must have a parent working in a GCC country with a valid Visa or Work Permit.
   - GCC Countries: Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, United Arab Emirates (UAE).
   - Proof required: Parent's Visa / Work Permit / Residence Permit valid during the admissions year.

3. **Foreign Nationals (FN):**
   - The student is a citizen of another country and has completed education abroad.
   - Proof required: Passport, educational records, visa documents of the student and passport of the parents.

Documents Required
------------------
- Passport (original + 2 photocopies) – student's passport
- OCI card (if applicable)
- Visa / Work Permit / Residence Permit of the parent (for CIWG)
- 10th Class (SSC) marks memo / equivalent
- 12th Class (Intermediate) marks memo / equivalent
- Transfer Certificate (TC)
- Migration Certificate (if applicable)
- Aadhar Card (photocopy) – if available
- Recent passport-size photographs (6 copies)
